Welcome to Our Institution

IMJISC, affiliated to Mangalore University, is a degree college established in the year 2022 in memory of the founder IM Jayaram Shetty. The institute offers Bachelor degree programmes in Commerce and Management (B.Com & BBA) and Computer Applications (BCA).These courses are reinforced by career oriented professional guidance & training and certificate courses. The academic progress of the students and their personality development are administered in tune with aspirations of National Education Policy 2020.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide accessible, quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in a dynamic global environment. We are committed to fostering critical thinking, innovation, and social responsibility through a comprehensive educational experience.

Vision

To be recognized as a leading institution that transforms lives through education, producing graduates who are intellectually competent, morally upright, and socially responsible. We aspire to create a community of lifelong learners who contribute positively to society and lead with integrity.
