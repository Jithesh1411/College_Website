Hostel

Our campus offers separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with modern amenities, 24x7 security, and a comfortable living environment conducive to learning.

Transportation

We provide reliable bus services covering all major areas of the city. Our fleet of buses ensures safe and punctual transportation for all students and staff.

Canteen

Our campus canteen serves fresh, nutritious meals at affordable prices. The menu is diverse, catering to various tastes and dietary requirements.

Laboratories

Our state-of-the-art laboratories are equipped with the latest equipment and technology to provide hands-on experience in various subjects including physics, chemistry, biology, and computer science.

Sports Area

We have extensive sports facilities including indoor and outdoor courts, a well-equipped gymnasium, a swimming pool, and training areas for various sports to encourage physical fitness and team spirit.

Library

Our spacious library houses an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources across various disciplines. It provides a quiet environment ideal for research and study.
